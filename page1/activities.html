Miami Beach

    3rd Street Beach Yoga

  • A beautiful way to start or end the day is by listening to the ocean crash and watching the sun rise or fall.
  • Cost: Free until 9am
  • Location: 3rd St & Ocean Drive, Miami Beach, FL 33139
  • Website: 3rdstreetbeachyoga.com

    South Pointe Park

  • A family-friendly park that is only a short walk away from the beautiful white sands of South Pointe Beach and the infamous pier.
  • Cost: Free
  • Location: 1 Washington Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33139
  • Website: miamiandbeaches.com

    Versace Mansion

  • Witness a hotspot in fashion history. This Villa was purchased by the famous Italian fashion designer, Gianni Versace where he was later murdered on the steps at the entrance. Today the Villa operates as a luxury boutique hotel, restaurant and event venue.
  • Cost: Prices Vary
  • Location: 1116 Ocean Dr, Miami Beach, FL 33139
  • Website: vmmiamibeach.com

    COMO Shambhala Urban Escape

  • This wellness Center provides a holistic escape from reality, with treatments from nuturing facials to deep tissue massages.
  • Cost: Prices Vary
  • Location: 2445 Collins Ave, Miami Beach, FL 33140
  • Website: comohotels.com

Downtown

    Bayfront Park

  • This park has a thing for everyone, from concert-lovers to those wanting a fun and new experience for family day.
  • Cost: Free
  • Location: 301 Biscayne Blvd, Miami, FL 33132
  • Website: bayfrontparkmiami.com

    Brickell City Centre

  • Style has no labels at the architectual beauty, Brickell City Centre, where you can shop your heart out or enjoy the amazing architecture.
  • Cost: Free until 9am
  • Location: 701 S Miami Ave. Miami, FL 33131
  • Website: brickellcitycentre.com

    Phillip and Patricia Frost Museum of Science

  • An adventurous wonder for kids of all ages, this interactive aquarium can act as a fun and educational day trip for any group.
  • Cost: Price Varies
  • Location: 1101 Biscayne Blvd, Miami, FL 33132
  • Website: frostscience.org

    Olympia Theater at Gusman Center for the Performing Arts

  • This cinematic treasure serves as a movie theater, concert venue and performing arts center and has been open since 1926.
  • Cost: Price Varies
  • Location: 174 E Flagler St, Miami, FL 33131
  • Website: olympiatheater.org

Coral Gables

  • Opened in 1924, this beautiful venue is a hotspot for toursists and locals trying to cool off after a hot Florida day.

  • Location: 2701 De Soto Blvd, Coral Gables, FL 33134
  • Website: coralgables.com

    Fairchild Tropical Botanic Garden

  • An 83-acre botanic garden, with extensive collections of rare tropical plants including palms, cycads, flowering trees, and vines.
  • Cost: Price Varies
  • Location: 10901 Old Cutler Rd, Coral Gables, FL 33156
  • Website: fairchildgarden.org

    Miracle Mile

  • One of South Florida's most sought-after shopping destinations, Miracle Mile features quality shops, art galleries, boutiques, restaurants and a live theater in a lushly landscaped environment of tree-lined streets.
  • Cost: Free
  • Location: Coral Way Ave. and S.W. 42nd Ave., Between S.W. 37th, Coral Gables, FL 33134
  • Website: shopcoralgables.com
